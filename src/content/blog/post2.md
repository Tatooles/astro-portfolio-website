Speedcubing is something you've probably never heard of. It is the 'sport' of
solving a Rubik's cube as fast as possbible. Currently the world record stands
at an incredible 3.05 seconds set by Xuanyi Geng in April 2025 at the age of 7
years old. Yes you read that correctly. You can see it for yourself
[here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tEONYcT9HOM).
